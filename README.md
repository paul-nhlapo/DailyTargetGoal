## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips for Maximum Impact

### Tip 1: The Reality Buffer
After week 1, add a 30-60 minute "buffer block" to your daily schedule. This accounts for inevitable interruptions and prevents constant rippling.

### Tip 2: Focus Mode Sprints
Schedule 90-minute Focus Mode blocks for deep work. The countdown creates urgency that boosts quality and speed.

### Tip 3: Interference Patterns
If Reality Audit shows consistent 2-3 PM interruptions, schedule low-focus tasks then. Save deep work for your "golden hours."

### Tip 4: Auto-Ripple Strategy
Schedule most important tasks early. If they overrun, Auto-Ripple pushes less critical afternoon tasks, not your priorities.

### Tip 5: Honest Logging
Log ALL interruptions over 3 minutes. The goal is brutal honesty, not perfection. Self-deception kills productivity.

### Tip 6: Weekly Optimization
Every Sunday, review your week's Reality Audits. Calculate "effective hours" (16 - interference). Aim to increase this weekly.
